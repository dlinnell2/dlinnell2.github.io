About Me

Diving head first into coding has been a long time coming. My earliest exposure to it came while I was in middle school, in a basic computer programming class. All HTML in notepad. Throughout high school and college it was something I would touch on occasionally, always as just an aside. Fast forward 5 years out of college, and, having grown tired of the hours of my previous job, I started looking for a change.


In January of 2018 I began a coding bootcamp in Houston. Come along and see what happens!
